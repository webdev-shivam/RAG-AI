## Company Overview

BrewGenius is a premium smart coffee and espresso machine manufacturer specializing in connected brewing technology. Founded in 2018 in Seattle, Washington, BrewGenius combines Italian craftsmanship with Silicon Valley innovation.

**Mission:** Deliver barista-quality coffee experiences through intelligent automation.

## Product Line Overview

BrewGenius offers three distinct product families:

1. **ProBarista Series (ThermoExtract™ Technology)** - Premium dual-boiler espresso machines with AI-powered extraction and touchscreen/app control
2. **SmartBrew Series (PrecisionDrip™ Technology)** - Connected drip coffee makers with programmable profiles and smart home integration
3. **GrindMaster Series** - Precision smart grinders with dosing technology

All espresso machines include **15-20 bar pressure systems** and **PID temperature control**.

### Daily
- Empty drip tray
- Rinse portafilter and basket

- Deep clean drip tray

- Wipe exterior

- Descale (or as indicated by machine)
- Clean water tank
- Inspect group head gasket
- Calibrate grinder (if needed)

### Every 6 Months
- Replace water filter (Elite)
- Deep clean grinder burrs
- Inspect steam wand tip

## WATER QUALITY GUIDELINES

### Recommended Water Specifications

| Parameter | Ideal Range |
|-----------|-------------|
| Total Hardness | 50-100 ppm |
| pH Level | 6.5-7.5 |
| Chlorine | 0 ppm |
| Total Dissolved Solids | 75-150 ppm |

### Water Quality Impact

| Water Type | Recommendation |
|------------|----------------|
| Tap (Soft) | Usually OK, test first |
| Tap (Hard) | Use filter or bottled |
| Distilled | NOT recommended (too pure, damages seals) |
| Reverse Osmosis | Add minerals back or avoid |
| Spring/Mineral (Bottled) | Ideal for most machines |

**Warning:** Using water outside recommended parameters may void warranty on boiler components.

## TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK REFERENCE

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Weak espresso | Grind too coarse | Adjust finer (2-3 steps) |
| Bitter espresso | Grind too fine / over-extraction | Adjust coarser, reduce time |
| No crema | Stale beans / wrong grind | Use fresh beans, adjust grind |
| Machine won't heat | Thermostat tripped | Unplug 30 min, restart |
| Slow flow | Scale buildup | Run descale cycle |
| Steam wand weak | Blocked tip | Soak tip in hot water, clear hole |
| App won't connect | WiFi issue | Reset machine WiFi, reconnect |
| Grinder jams | Oily beans stuck | Clean burrs, use less oily beans |
| Loud pump noise | Air in system | Prime pump, check water tank |

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need a separate grinder with ProBarista machines?**
A: No, all ProBarista machines include built-in grinders. However, some enthusiasts prefer a separate premium grinder (like GrindMaster Pro) for maximum control and consistency.

**Q: Can I use pre-ground coffee?**
A: Yes, all machines accept pre-ground coffee through the bypass chute. However, we recommend grinding fresh for best flavor (coffee goes stale within 15 minutes of grinding).

**Q: Are machines compatible with pods/capsules?**
A: No, BrewGenius machines are designed for fresh coffee beans and ground coffee only. We don't support pod systems.

**Q: What's the difference between ThermoExtract and PrecisionDrip?**
A: ThermoExtract is our espresso technology using 9-15 bar pressure and AI-optimized temperature curves. PrecisionDrip is our drip coffee technology using gravity and precise temperature control for optimal extraction.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Can ProBarista machines be plumbed in?**
A: No, all models use removable water tanks only. Direct water line connection is not available.

**Q: What electrical requirements are needed?**
A: All machines use standard 120V outlets. ProBarista Elite and Studio should have dedicated circuits (not shared with other high-draw appliances). No special installation required.

**Q: How loud are the grinders?**
A: GrindMaster Pro: 68 dB (quieter than a vacuum). GrindMaster Basic: 72 dB. Built-in grinders: 70-75 dB. Grinding takes 5-15 seconds per dose.

**Q: Can I adjust pressure for different beans?**
A: Yes, ProBarista Elite and Studio allow pressure profiling from 9-15 bar. Compact uses fixed 15 bar.

### App & Connectivity

**Q: What smart home systems work with BrewGenius?**
A: SmartBrew Series: Full compatibility with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri Shortcuts. ProBarista Series: App control only (voice assistant integration coming 2026 Q3).

**Q: Does the app work without WiFi?**
A: Basic machine controls work via Bluetooth (within 30 feet). Advanced features like scheduling, usage statistics, and remote start require WiFi.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve our products. Personal brewing data stays on your device. You can opt out of analytics in app settings.

### Purchasing & Support

**Q: Do you offer financing?**
A: Yes, through Affirm at checkout. 0% APR available on purchases over $500 for qualified buyers (6 or 12 month terms).

**Q: Can I return if I don't like it?**
A: Yes, 30-day return policy. Product must be in original packaging with all accessories. Customer responsible for return shipping. Refund excludes original shipping cost.

**Q: Where are BrewGenius products made?**
A: ProBarista machines are assembled in Italy using components from Italy, Germany, and Switzerland. SmartBrew and GrindMaster products are designed in Seattle and manufactured in Taiwan.

**Q: Do you have showrooms?**
A: Yes, flagship showrooms in Seattle (HQ), San Francisco, New York, and Chicago. Additionally, select Williams-Sonoma and Sur La Table locations carry demo units.

## GLOSSARY

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Bar** | Unit of pressure (1 bar = 14.5 PSI). Espresso typically requires 9 bar. |
| **PID** | Proportional-Integral-Derivative controller for precise temperature regulation |
| **Thermoblock** | Heating system that heats water on-demand (faster warm-up, less consistent) |
| **Dual Boiler** | Separate boilers for brewing and steaming (allows simultaneous use) |
| **Portafilter** | Handle with basket that holds ground coffee for espresso extraction |
| **Tamping** | Compressing ground coffee in the portafilter before extraction |
| **Extraction** | Process of pulling flavor from coffee using water and pressure |
| **Crema** | Golden foam layer on properly extracted espresso |
| **Bloom** | Pre-wetting coffee grounds to release CO2 before full extraction |
| **Channeling** | Uneven extraction from water finding paths of least resistance |
| **Dosing** | Measuring the amount of coffee grounds used per shot |
| **TDS** | Total Dissolved Solids - measure of water mineral content |
